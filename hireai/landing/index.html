FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Will clients know they're talking to an AI? ▼
Your agent introduces itself as a "digital assistant for [Your Agency]". It's professional and transparent without disrupting the conversation flow. You can also fully customise how it introduces itself.
What happens if the AI can't handle a conversation? ▼
Anytime a client shows frustration or asks something complex, the agent immediately escalates to you with full context, flags it in your dashboard, and sends you a notification. You take over seamlessly.
How long does setup take? ▼
Our guided onboarding wizard takes 5–10 minutes. You'll connect your channels, upload your listings, and launch your agent. Most customers are live the same day they sign up.
Can I use my own phone number for WhatsApp? ▼
Yes. HireAI integrates with Twilio, which supports WhatsApp Business API. You can use an existing number or get a new one through Twilio. Setup instructions are included in the onboarding wizard.
Is my client data secure? ▼
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never share your client data with third parties. Each agency's data is fully isolated.
