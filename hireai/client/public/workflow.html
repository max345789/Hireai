Key Stages at a Glance
1
Lead Entry
Lead contacts via WhatsApp, Email, Web Chat, Instagram DM, or Facebook Messenger.
2
AI Processing
Claude AI reads the message, loads lead history, and decides the best response action.
3
Intent Detection
AI classifies intent — wants info, ready to book, upset, or needs clarification.
4
Booking Flow
Checks Google Calendar availability, creates booking, and sends confirmation automatically.
5
Escalation
Negative sentiment or complex questions trigger an alert — AI pauses, human takes over.
6
Human Handoff
Agent responds manually. After resolution, AI is handed back with full context intact.
7
Follow-up Engine
Cron job sweeps every 30 min. Sends auto follow-ups to non-responsive leads.
8
Analytics & ROI
All actions update the dashboard in real-time. ROI, funnel, and heatmaps tracked live.
